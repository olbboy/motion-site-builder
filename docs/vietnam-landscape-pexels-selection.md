# Vietnam Landscape Collection — Pexels Media Selection

Twenty landscape-oriented Pexels photos selected for the 20-prompt Vietnam collection. Each prompt uses a stable local asset target and keeps the Pexels photo page as the provenance record.

## Asset workflow

1. Open the Pexels source page and download the original or a landscape rendition at least 2400 px wide.
2. Save it to the exact local target below. Do not hotlink `images.pexels.com` from the generated site.
3. Preserve the specified focal point when making responsive crops.
4. Re-check the current [Pexels license](https://www.pexels.com/license/) before publication.
5. Do not sell or redistribute an unaltered photo as a standalone print/product, and do not imply that a visible person, property, or brand endorses the generated concept. The Karst & Canvas prompt therefore requires a material graphic transformation and offers no photo-only product.
